Buckwheat

Description

Cooked buckwheat is used as garnish for other meals like meat or fish. It's used a lot in Eastern Europe, especially in the East Slavic countries. Buckwheat is also consumed in Poland, and in Lithuania they sometimes make pancakes from it.

Ingredients

  • raw buckwheat (usually sold in big enough packs)

Steps

  1. Put 1 cup of buckwheat into a pot and rinse it well, a couple of times
  2. Drain the water
  3. Add 2.5 of clear water
  4. Put the pot on medium fire
  5. Let it boil for 5-7 minutes
  6. Take it off the stove and let it sit for 15-20 minutes
