Okroshka

Description

Okroshka is a traditional Ukrainian soup that is served cold. It's usually made on the basis of either whey or kvass. It includes a mixture of fresh vegetables and sausages. Okroshka is normally a summer dish and is often served at Easter.

Ingredients

  • potatoes - 4
  • eggs - 4
  • sausage - 250g
  • cucumbers - 2-3
  • kefir - 1l
  • sparkling water - 1 bottle
  • dill
  • green onion OR half a normal onion
  • OPTIONAL: sour cream
  • OPTIONAL: citric acid

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes for 20 minutes
  2. Boil eggs for 10 minutes
  3. Cut into cubes: potatoes, eggs, sausage, cucumbers as well as finely chop onion and dill and put it all into a pan
  4. OPTIONAL: add a couple of table spoons of sour cream
  5. Pour kefir into the pan
  6. Mix and add salt to taste
  7. Dilute with sparkling water, the consistency should be mainly watery, slightly thickish
  8. Taste for salt, if not enough -- add to taste
  9. OPTIONAL: taste for citric acid, if not enough -- add to taste
