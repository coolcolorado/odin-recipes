Okroshka

Description

Okroshka is a traditional Ukrainian soup that is served cold. It's usually made on the basis of either whey or kvass. It includes a mixture of fresh vegetables and sausages. Okroshka is normally a summer dish and is often served at Easter.

Ingredients

  • potatoes - 4
  • eggs - 4
  • sausage - 250g
  • cucumbers - 2-3
  • kefir - 1l
  • sparkling water - 1 bottle
  • dill
  • green onion OR half a normal onion
  • OPTIONAL: sour cream
  • OPTIONAL: citric acid
