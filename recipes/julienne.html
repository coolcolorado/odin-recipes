Julienne

Description

Julienne is a popular dish of the Soviet restaurant cuisine. The name comes from the French term for cutting fresh veggies but eventually the term lost its original meaning. Julienne is made of mushrooms, cheese, and chicken to name the most important ingredients.

Ingredients

  • chopped mushrooms - 1kg
  • medium sized onions - 5
  • one chicken breast or two halves
  • sour cream
  • cheese - 200g
  • OPTIONAL: flour - 1 table spoon

Steps

  1. Quarter onions and slice the quarters
  2. Fry for 10 minutes (with oil) before it becomes gold-ish
  3. Put in cubed chicken breasts, add salt, pepper, seasoning
  4. Fry for 10-15 minutes
  5. Put in mushrooms, mix, fry for 10 minutes
  6. Sprinkle flours, mix in 3 minute
  7. Mix 200-400g of sour cream and water in a bowl
  8. Pour into the pan, let it boil for 5 minutes
  9. Taste for salt, add if needed
  10. Add more water of sour cream if it's too thick or watery
  11. Grate cheese and put it on top, let it sit for 10 minutes
  12. Now you can turn off the stove, let the dish sit off the stove for 20 minutes before eating

*Everything should be fried on medium fire or a bit less
